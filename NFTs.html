What is an NFT?

NFT stands for Nonfungible token. This means that the NFT itself is unique and cant be traded for the same thing. NFTs are digital art pieces that can be bought on an exchange like open sea. Think of it like traditional art pieces but integrated into the Ethereum blockchain, so you know you own the original. When you exchange your crypto for an nft, you buy the ownership token that confirms that you own it. Now, what stops people from just downloading the jpeg and calling it a day. Well, you can make the same argument for traditional paintings where you search up a picture of the Mona Lisa and you print it off. The original will always be the one that has intrinsic value.

Potential Of NFTs

The potential of NFTs is limitless. NFTs act like a certificate of ownership that's logged on the blockchain. In the future, we will see houses being bought by purchasing or perhaps even financing an NFT. Right now there are also special clubs that only include members that own an NFT from their collection. These clubs will have public meet-ups and talk about NFTs and the whole crypto space. Right now NFTs might seem like a novelty item, but as we advance into more software focussed technology ecosystems, I have no doubt that NFTs will be used for a variety of things. From tickets to purchasing ownership of items, this technology will disrupt the tech sector and how we deal with transactions.
