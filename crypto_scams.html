Is Crypto Just A Scam?

Research has shown that if you invest 5-10% of your portfolio into the top 10 cryptocurrencies, your return will be more than if you didn't. To make sure you don't fall victim to crypto scams stay away from meme coins that have no differentiating utility or quality unless you're ok with potentially losing money. You should also avoid coins that are brand new or prevent you from selling. As long as you follow these guidelines you are highly unlikely to fall victim to scams.
