History Of Crypto

Bitcoin launched in 2008 amid the recession to provide people with an alternative investment. The creator of Bitcoin is known under the name Satoshi Nakamoto. His creation would inspire people to look for better alternatives. At a time when inflation was at an all-time high Bitcoin promised to be a hedge against inflation because of its limited quantity as a deflationary currency. After the word had gotten out, several more altcoins(other coins) emerged to improve on bitcoin or to look at it from a different point of view altogether. Some notable coins are Ethereum, Litecoin, Dogecoin, Solana, XRP, etc. In 2022 we have seen the emergence of meme coins; which are highly volatile cryptocurrencies with their price depending on the people backing them. Some of these meme coins have turned out to be scams and cash grabs for their creators. As we move on into the future, crypto will become more integrated into our lives and maybe someday be our primary currency around the globe.
